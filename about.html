Growing Up

Stefani Joanne Angelina Germanotta "Lady Gaga" was born on March 28, 1986, in New York City to Cynthia Louise and Joseph Germanotta. She began playing the piano at the age of 4. From age 11, she attended the Convent of the Sacred Heart. Gaga described herself in school as, "... a misfit among her peers." The lessons taught her to create music by ear, which she preferred over reading sheet music. Her parents encouraged her to pursue music, and enrolled her in Creative Arts Camp. She also studied method acting at the Lee Strasberg Theatre and Film Institute.

The Story of Gaga

During the second semester of her sophomore year in 2005, she withdrew to focus on her music.

The Beginning of it All

From 2008-2010, Gaga let the world know who she was and made her mark with The Fame Monster. Then, from 2011-2014, she presented the world with: Born This Way, Artpop, and Cheek to Cheek. From 2015-2017, she participated in American Horror Story, released Joanne, and performed at the Super Bowl.

2018 is a Year to Remember

In March 2018, Gaga supported a gun-control rally in Washington DC and released a cover of Elton John's "Your Song" for his tribute album Revamp. Later in the year, she starred as a struggling singer named Ally in Bradley Cooper's critically acclaimed film A Star Is Born, a remake of the 1937 film of the same name. Gaga and Cooper co-wrote and produced most of the songs on the soundtrack for A Star Is Born, which she insisted they perform live in the film. Its lead single, "Shallow ", performed by the two, was released in September and has reached number one in Australia, Austria, Ireland, New Zealand, Sweden, Switzerland, and the UK as well as number five in the US. The soundtrack's release coincided with the film's, and it contains 34 tracks, including 19 original songs. Gaga also has signed a two-year residency, named Lady Gaga Enigma, to perform at the MGM Park Theater in Las Vegas, which is scheduled to begin in December 2018. She has also started working on her sixth studio album.
